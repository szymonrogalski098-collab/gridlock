What is Gridlock?

Gridlock is a browser-based arcade dodge game played on a 16×16 neon grid. Lasers fire, blocks fall, and you have one job: don't die.

Dash up to 5 cells to dodge incoming threats, survive as many rounds as possible, earn coins, unlock skins, and — if you're good enough — tear a rift into World 2: The Void.


How to Play

Controls


Click a cell — dash to that cell (max 5 cells away)
Survive the round — stay alive until the gap phase to earn coins
Combo — survive 5 rounds in a row for a bonus


Core mechanics


Lasers charge (semi-transparent warning) then fire — be off the cell when they light up
Blocks fall with a warning — don't be under them
Dash — click any cell within 5 tiles to teleport there instantly
Combo system — chain rounds for bonus coins; reach ×20 to trigger GRIDLOCK MODE


GRIDLOCK MODE

Activate at combo ×20: glitch visuals, chromatic aberration, scanlines, screen shake, and 2× coins for 5 rounds.

World 2 — The Void

Defeat the VOID KING (round 100, Normal/Hard) to unlock World 2. A cosmic dimension with:


Solar Flares instead of lasers — they charge up and fire as wide beams
Asteroids — fly across the board in 8 directions with fire trails
Black Hole — click far away to teleport instead of dashing
Void Crystals — separate currency earned only in The Void
New bosses — PULSAR, NEUTRON, SINGULARITY with active combat mechanics
